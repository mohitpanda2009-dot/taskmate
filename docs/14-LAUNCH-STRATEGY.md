**Launch Task Category: "Stand In Line For You"**
- Target: Pune's RTO offices, passport offices, municipal corporation
- Marketing: "Why waste 4 hours at the RTO? TaskMate: â‚¹199"

**Expand quickly to:**

**Strategy 2: Housing Society Partnerships**

- Partner with 20-30 large housing societies in Pune
- Become the "official task board" for the society
- Replace physical notice boards with TaskMate
- Society-specific deals: "First task free for [Society Name] residents"
- WhatsApp broadcast to society WhatsApp groups

**Strategy 3: Office Complex Partnerships**

- Target IT parks: Hinjewadi, Kharadi, EON Free Zone
- "TaskMate for your office": get documents, run errands, pickup deliveries
- B2B angle: companies can create "office tasks" (courier pickup, pantry supplies)

**Strategy 4: Seed Tasks (Create Your Own Demand)**

- For first 2-4 weeks, TaskMate team posts real tasks themselves
- Ensures doers have work from day 1
- Builds rating/review data
- Creates "proof of concept" screenshots for marketing
